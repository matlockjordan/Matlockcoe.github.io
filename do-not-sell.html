Do Not Sell My Personal Information

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Opt-Out

California residents have the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of their personal information. Although we do not sell personal information in the traditional sense, California law defines “sale” broadly and may include certain information sharing for analytics or marketing.

To exercise your right to opt out, please submit the form below or email us at jordan@matlockcoe.com. We will honor verified requests within 15 business days.

Submitting opens your email app to send the request. You may also email us directly.

Verification & Recordkeeping

We may request additional information to verify your identity. We will keep records of opt-out requests for at least 24 months.

Under 16

We do not knowingly sell personal information of consumers under 16 years of age. If you believe this has occurred, please contact us.
