About

Why we started and what we stand for.

Our Story

Founded by Jordan Matlock, Matlock Center of Excellence LLC started with a simple idea: help people and organizations reach their full potential. With years of experience leading teams, solving complex logistics challenges, and driving operational improvements, we bring a hands-on, practical approach to problem-solving.

Values

  • Integrity — do the right thing, clearly and consistently.
  • Innovation — test, learn, and iterate toward better outcomes.
  • Continuous Improvement — small changes compound into big results.
  • Empowerment — teach tools and frameworks you can reuse.

Approach

We meet you where you are, explain everything in plain language, and make sure you have practical next steps — no jargon, no pressure.
